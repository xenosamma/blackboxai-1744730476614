Types of E-Waste We Accept

We responsibly recycle a comprehensive range of electronic equipment, from consumer devices to specialized industrial equipment.

Consumer Electronics

Smartphones, tablets, laptops, computers, and home entertainment systems.

Medical Equipment

Diagnostic devices, monitoring equipment, and medical imaging systems.

Telecommunications

Network equipment, transmission systems, and communication devices.
